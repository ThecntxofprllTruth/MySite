The Local Group is the galaxy group that includes the Milky Way. It contains over 54 galaxies, including Andromeda, Triangulum, and the Magellanic Clouds.

These galaxies are gravitationally bound to each other, and their center of mass is located somewhere between the Milky Way and Andromeda galaxies.

For more information, visit: Wikipedia - Local Group

Biodiversity refers to the variety of life on Earth at all its levels, from genes to ecosystems, and can encompass the evolutionary, ecological, and cultural processes that sustain life.
